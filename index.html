They do not sweat and whine about their condition;
They do not lie awake in the dark and weep for their sins;

Our History

CENTRAL zoo is one of the biggest zoo in the town. The land area of the zoo is 33 acres. CENTRAL zoo was established in the year 1980 with 10 acre land but lately it was modified and land area was also added in the year 2000. Proper residing space for all the type of animals has been made and maintains human security at high rate. Proper supply of food for all the animals is maintained and checked at regular intervals. The predecessor of the central zoo is the menagerie, which has a long history from the ancient world to modern times.
